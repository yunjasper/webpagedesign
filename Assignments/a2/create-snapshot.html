How to capture a snapshot of a webpage?

Here are the steps:

  1. Maximize the window you want to capture
  2. In the menu of your browser, choose View|Full screen
  3. Press the key "Print Screen" on your keyboard
  4. Open an image editor (Paint may be convenient)
  5. Paste what you just copied
  6. If necessary, crop the picture
  7. Save it as a jpg file
  8. Use it!
Now click on the Assignment tab (or window) to continue!
